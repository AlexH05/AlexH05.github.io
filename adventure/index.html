    July 21, 2016 (afternoon)

    Map of Universal
  2. Once we got into the Park we had to take a picture of the Universal globe. . Our next stop was in the Jurassic Park section. We first had to get a picture with Blue before we could go on any rides. Then the Jurrasic Park ride came and we got soaked.     3. July 21, 2016 (evening)

  3. The next place we stopped was Krustyland. Here there were many fun games, rides, and even shows. We ate some more food at Moes and then went on the Jaws ride which was fun later in the day. We saw it was going to get dark so we went on the studio tour before that closed to the night. Then we finished up our night with the Universal city walk which was so fun to look at in the night time and grabbed a giant donut for a nightime snack.
