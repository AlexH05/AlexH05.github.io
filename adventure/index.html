My Universal Adventure

July 21, 2016(morning)

Today we went to universal in California. We first got up early and ate a big coninental breakfast at the hotel. Next we got dressed and ready for the drive down there. We drove our rental car and got there just in time for open. We got there early so we could spend the whole day there and get our money's worth.

July 21, 2016 (afternoon)

Once we got into the Park we had to take a picture of the universal globe. IMAGE Our next stop was in the Jurassic Park section. July 21, 2016 (evening)

The next place we stopped was Krustyland. IMAGE Here there were many fun games, rides, and even shows. We ate some more food at Moes and then went on the Jaws ride which was fun later in the day.
